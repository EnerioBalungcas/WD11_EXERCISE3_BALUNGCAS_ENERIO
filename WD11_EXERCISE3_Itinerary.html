Suggested Itinerary

Entry Point: Either Airport, Seaport or Land Trip
  • Day 1 - Arrival at airport or seaport; Take Tricycle or your pre-arranged transport to your hotel; Explore Dumaguete City on foot (i.e. Malls, Restaurants, Street Foods, Landmarks)
  • Day 2 - First stop: Valencia Town; Second stop: Dauin - Bedjao Viewing Deck; Third stop: Malatapay seaside restaurants (Launch will be served); Fourth stop: Apo Island (Dinner will be served)
  • Day 3 - First stop: Pulang Bato Falls; Second stop: Dauin - Balinsasayaw Twin Lakes (Lunch will be served); Third stop: Canlaon Gardens, upon return (Dinner will be served at Dumaguete Boulevard);
  • Day 4 - First stop: Canlaon Gardens - this is a 1-day trip. Lunch will be served there. Upon return (Dinner will be served at Dumaguete Boulevard);
