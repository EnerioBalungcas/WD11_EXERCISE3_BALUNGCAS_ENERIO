ABOUT

Location
Negros Oriental is a province situated in Western Visayas region and occupies the southeastern part of Negros Island, facing Cebu from the east.
Capital City
Dumaguete is dubbed as the "City of Gentle People". It has airport, seaport, shopping malls, hotels, restaurants and publics parks.
Language
Cebuano is the main dialect but English and Tagalog are widely spoken.
Transportation
You can reach Dumaguete in any mode of transport either by air, sea or land. Tricycle is the main mood of public within the city and towns. Buses and Jeepneys are also available for long-distance trips.
Cuisine
Dumaguete is not short of cuisines(i.e. Filipino or Euroupean cuisines). Local foods and delicacies like Pork Adobo, Chicken Adobo, roasted pig(lechon) are common everywhere.
